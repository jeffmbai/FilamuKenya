Filamu254 is a movie displaying site that has all the types of movie, TV shows and commedies of your interest. 

filamu254 website has been developed using some of the current web design languages which are : current version of hypertext markup language(HTML5), cascading style sheet(CSS3),hypertext preprocessor(PHP7),structured query language(SQL) and Javascript.

Filamu254 has various pages which have been incorporated together to achieve a movie site.

The first page to interact with as a new user is the index page which has a very simplified interface for our users.
As a new user when you access the index page, the first thing you need to do is to sign up and create your own account.
To sign up just click on users that will give you a dropdown menu with sign up optionthe sign up link which is along the navigation bar.

Immediately after signup you will be redirected to login page. Since you now have an account, you can login immediately or at your own time of will.
When you login, you will be redirected to your account from where you can now watch any movie of your interest. While watching the movie, our site will suggest other related movies to you that you can also watch and like.

Site admnistrator can login as an admin and redirected to the dashboard from where you can post movies, tv shows and trailers,view messages sent by site visitors and do site analytics.

Site visitors can communicate with us via the contact form page.

Site visitors can subscribe with us for more movies via the subscription page on their personal home page.
